POWER OUTAGES IMFORMATION

At the CEB, we improve our electricity network by carefully planning power cuts. The power is off while our crews are working to make the network safe for you and your equipment.

We do our best to keep the outage time to a minimum and thank you for your patience while we know it is difficult to live without electricity in this busy world.

Despite the expected time for planned outages, the power can be restored slightly before or after the scheduled time depending on the site conditions.

Unplanned outages occur due to faults and are beyond CEB’s control. Our crews are doing the best to speed up the repairs and minimize the outage time. We’ll switch on the power, without further notice, once the repairs are over.
